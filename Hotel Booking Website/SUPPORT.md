# Support Policy & Guidelines — The Grand Horizon Template

Thank you for purchasing **The Grand Horizon Hotel & Suites Template**! We are dedicated to delivering top-tier customer support and assisting you with template customization.

---

## What Support Includes

- **Bug Fixes**: Resolution of any unexpected browser bugs, HTML syntax issues, or CSS layout glitches.
- **Template Clarification**: Answering questions about template features, setup, and file structure.
- **Documentation Assistance**: Helping you locate specific settings within `css/variables.css` or `js/data.js`.

---

## What Support Does NOT Include

- **Custom Feature Development**: Creating custom back-end booking engines, server integrations, or database connections.
- **Third-Party Script Modifications**: Installing or troubleshooting external 3rd party plugins not shipped with this template.
- **Hosting / Domain Setup**: Configuring your web hosting server, cPanel, or DNS settings.

---

## How to Get Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions:
1. Review the offline documentation in `Documentation/index.html`.
2. Check `js/data.js` and `css/variables.css` for configuration parameters.
3. Submit a ticket or message through your marketplace item support tab.

Our response time is typically within **24 hours** on business days.
